How to Organize a Vaccine Clinic

Find a local vaccine clinic below!

https://www.vaccines.gov/search/

☐ Contact a local vaccine clinic to see if they are interested in an off-site clinic

Once you are in contact with a clinic, work with them to schedule the event. Make sure to help facilitate all their needs including, but limited to:

☐ Choosing a date and time

☐ Finding a location by contacting potential venues. Opt for smaller, appointment-only clinics. These can be more effective and protect the health of staff and patients. be sure to check with your provider that the space meets their needs.
